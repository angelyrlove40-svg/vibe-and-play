# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want VibeMUD to be a welcoming project for people who enjoy terminal tools, local-first software, and playful developer workflows.

Participants are expected to be respectful, constructive, and considerate of different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Doxing or sharing private information
- Discriminatory language or conduct
- Repeated off-topic disruption
- Publishing security-sensitive details before maintainers can respond

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community. Security issues should be reported privately as described in `SECURITY.md`.
